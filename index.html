Frequently Asked Questions

Is Char Dham Yatra safe for senior citizens?

Yes, when planned with patience, realistic pacing, proper rest breaks, and medical guidance. We specialize in senior-friendly itineraries with altitude acclimatization and verified stays.

What is included and not included in the package?

We provide a detailed inclusion and exclusion list before booking. Typically includes: verified accommodation, transport, select meals, guidance. Excludes: personal expenses, optional activities, travel insurance.

Do you help with registration and documentation?

Yes, we guide you step-by-step for online registration, required documents (ID proof, medical certificate if needed), and weather updates before departure.

What is the best time to visit Char Dham?

May-June and September-early October are ideal. Weather is pleasant, roads are safe, and crowd levels are manageable. Avoid July-August monsoon season.

Can we customize the itinerary?

Absolutely. We understand every senior has different comfort levels. We customize pace, add rest days, upgrade accommodation, and adjust based on your health requirements.
